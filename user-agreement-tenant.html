USER AGREEMENT - TENANT

ARTICLE 1
The Construction Team and Extent of Agreement THE Tenant accepts the relationship of trust and confidence established between him and the Owner by this Agreement. Tenant agrees to furnish the construction services set forth herein and agrees to furnish efficient business administration and superintendence, and to use his best efforts to complete the Project in the best and soundest way and in the most expeditious and economical manner consistent with the interests of the Owner.
1.1 The Construction Team: The Tenant and the Owner, called the “Construction Team” shall work from the beginning of the Project through construction completion.
1.2 Extent of Agreement: This Agreement represents the entire agreement between the Owner and the Tenant and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations or agreements. This Agreement shall not be superseded by any provisions of the documents for construction and may be amended only by written instrument signed by both Owner and Tenant.
1.3 Definitions: The Project is the total construction of the Building, constructed per the Drawings and Specifications of which the Work is a part. The Work comprises the completed construction required by the Drawings and Specifications. The term day shall mean calendar day unless otherwise specifically designated.

ARTICLE 2
Tenant’s Responsibilities
2.1 Tenant’s Services
2.1.1 The Tenant shall be responsible for furnishing the construction of the Project. The Owner and Tenant shall develop a construction phase schedule and the Owner shall be responsible for prompt decisions and approvals so as to maintain the approved schedule.
2.1.2 The Tenant will secure permits necessary for the construction of the Project.

2.2 Responsibilities With Respect to Construction
2.2.1 The Tenant will provide all construction supervision, inspection, labor, materials, tools, construction equipment and subcontracted items necessary for the execution and completion of the Project.
2.2.2 The Tenant will pay all sales, use, gross receipts and similar taxes related to the Work provided by the Tenant which have been legally enacted at the time of execution of this Agreement and for which the Tenant is liable.
2.2.3 The Tenant will prepare and submit for the Owner’s approval an estimated progress schedule for the Project. This schedule shall indicate the dates for the starting and completion of the various stages of the design and construction. It shall be revised as required by the conditions of the Work and those conditions and events which are beyond the Tenant’s control.
2.2.4 The Tenant shall at all times keep the premises free from the accumulation of waste materials or rubbish caused by his operations. At the completion of the Work, he shall remove all of his waste material and rubbish from and around the Project as well as all his tools, construction equipment, machinery and surplus materials.
2.2.5 The Tenant will give all notices and comply with all laws and ordinances legally enacted at the date of execution of the Agreement, which govern the proper execution of the Work.
