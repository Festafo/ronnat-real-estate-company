WELCOME TO RONNAT

Our company, RONNAT, provides on-site industrial building solutions to the entire market across the globe for short, medium, or long term periods. We also develop domestic solutions to the taste of our amazing customers like YOU. These solutions are available for rent or outright purchase.

We offer complete project management solution. Our activities involve requirements engineering, complete process modelling, business case preparation, stakeholder management, clients' interfacing, execution monitoring, budget controlling, user acceptance testing and design engineering. We do all these using lifecycle costing with usability in mind.
